01
The split-stay idea

Two hotels, one trip — a base for exploring, then a base for doing nothing.

Staying in one place means long drives to every attraction. Splitting the stay puts you next to the action first, then moves you to a calmer stretch of coast to wind down — and the South-West tour happens naturally on the day you switch.

Part 1 · Touring base

The North — Grand Baie

3 nights · 12–15 Dec · Veranda Grand Baie

The island's lively hub — the catamaran pier, Port Louis and the famous botanical garden are all on your doorstep. A good-value beachfront hotel here means short transfers and full tour days.

  • Walking distance to restaurants, shops & the boat pier
  • 30–45 min from Port Louis & Pamplemousses garden
  • Family rooms, kids' club, two pools — easy on the budget
  • Prettier beaches (Trou aux Biches, Pereybere) are minutes away
Tour day
on the move
Part 2 · Resort & unwind

The West — Flic en Flac

4 nights · 15–19 Dec · Sugar Beach

The longest white-sand beach, a calm reef-protected lagoon and the island's best sunsets. This is where the days slow down — big pool, beach, water sports and a Casela adventure nearby.

  • Long, safe, shallow lagoon — perfect for the kids
  • Multiple restaurants, spa, kids' club
  • 20 min from Casela World of Adventures & dolphin bay
  • 50 min from the airport on departure day
Want it more relaxed? Casela (Day 5) is a half-day, so you can drop it and have 3 tour days + 3 full chill days instead. Every tour is optional and easily swapped.

Day by day

The eight-day itinerary.

Sightseeing in coral; unwind in lagoon; travel & hotel-move in clay and teal.

Travel / transfer Sightseeing Hotel move Beach & chill
1

Touchdown in paradise

Sat 12 Dec · Arrival · MRU → Grand Baie
Travel

Clear immigration, pick up the pre-booked minibus and drive north (~65 km, just over an hour). Check in to Veranda Grand Baie, unpack, pool time. The only goal today is a sunset walk and an easy dinner in Grand Baie village.

Dinner idea: dozens of seafront restaurants within a 5-minute walk — no reservation needed on arrival night.
2

Catamaran to the Northern Isles

Sun 13 Dec · Full day · Coin de Mire · Gabriel · Flat Island
Sightseeing Sea day

Board a catamaran right from Grand Baie and sail to the protected islets offshore — the easiest, most magical family day: calm clear water, BBQ lunch on deck, snorkelling over coral gardens.

  • Snorkelling off Îlot Gabriel — turtles, parrotfish, shallow & safe
  • BBQ lunch & open bar served onboard
  • Beach time on Flat Island's white sand
3

The North by road — garden, sugar & the capital

Mon 14 Dec · Full day · Pamplemousses · Port Louis · Cap Malheureux
Sightseeing

A driver-guide (or self-drive) loop of the north's classics — giant waterlilies at the botanical garden, Mauritian history at the sugar estate, the colours of Port Louis market, and the postcard red-roof chapel.

  • SSR Botanic Garden, Pamplemousses — giant waterlilies, giant tortoises
  • Port Louis — Caudan Waterfront, Central Market, Citadel
  • Cap Malheureux — red-roof Notre Dame, sunset over the islets
4

Volcano, sacred lake & seven-coloured earth

Tue 15 Dec · Full day · Check out → South-West → Flic en Flac
Sightseeing Hotel move

The clever bit: today's tour runs on the route between your two hotels. Check out, then a driver-guide takes you through the lush centre and dramatic south-west — crater, the Hindu pilgrimage lake with its 33 m Shiva statue, the Black River Gorges and the surreal rainbow sands of Chamarel — finishing at Sugar Beach for a sunset swim.

  • Trou aux Cerfs — extinct volcano crater, panoramic views
  • Grand Bassin (Ganga Talao) — sacred lake & giant Shiva statue
  • Chamarel — Seven Coloured Earth, waterfall & rum tasting
Long driving day — have the driver hold your bags and leave straight after breakfast.
5

Casela — safari, zip-lines & giraffes

Wed 16 Dec · Half day · Cascavelle (20 min from hotel)
Sightseeing Afternoon at leisure

The one outing that thrills a 9- and a 12-year-old. Casela is part safari park, part adventure playground — a bus safari among zebras & ostriches, feeding giraffes, a petting farm and zip-lines. Back to the resort by early afternoon for pool & beach.

  • Safari bus through the African-savanna enclosures
  • Zip-lines & Nepalese bridge for the kids (limits apply)
6

Do-nothing day (with an optional dolphin dawn)

Thu 17 Dec · At leisure · Sugar Beach, Flic en Flac
Beach & chill

A proper resort day — kayaks and paddleboards on the lagoon, kids' club, a long lunch and an unhurried afternoon under a parasol. Early risers can book a 7 am speedboat dolphin trip from Tamarin Bay (20 min) and be back for a late breakfast.

  • Optional: dawn dolphin swim at Tamarin Bay
  • Sunset: Flic en Flac faces dead west — the best on the island
7

Last full day — make it count, slowly

Fri 18 Dec · At leisure · Sugar Beach, Flic en Flac
Beach & chill

A repeat of the best bits — beach, pool, one last spa session. For a finale, an optional sunset catamaran past Crystal Rock and the basalt cliffs of Le Morne, drinks onboard as the sun drops. Otherwise, a long farewell dinner at the resort.

  • Optional: west-coast sunset catamaran
8

One last swim, then homeward

Sat 19 Dec · Departure · Flic en Flac → MRU
Travel

A slow breakfast and a final dip before check-out. The airport is only ~50 min from Flic en Flac — leave with a comfortable buffer. Land back in the UAE the same evening, same time zone, no jet lag to spoil the week.

Getting there & around

Island logistics, solved for a group of eight.

Direct flights from Dubai/Sharjah take about six hours — Mauritius shares the UAE's time zone, so no jet lag. On-island, the relaxed move for eight is a single driver-guide van on tour days plus pre-booked minibus transfers.

One van, one driver

For eight, hiring a driver-guide with an 8-seater van for the three land tour days (and transfers) beats self-driving or rebooking taxis. Everyone rides together, nobody navigates, cost splits three ways.

~800 AED / van / day
  • Door-to-door on tour days, no parking stress
  • Local knowledge — best photo stops & lunch spots
  • Carry your luggage on the hotel-move day for free
  • Book the airport minibus with the same operator for a discount

Before you go

Practical essentials.

Everything worth knowing at a glance — most of it pleasantly in your favour from the UAE.

Weather

Early summer — ~27–31°C, warm & humid with brief showers (often overnight). Sea ~27°C. Cyclone season runs Nov–May but mid-December risk is low.

Visas & entry

UAE passport holders: visa-free up to 90 days. Residents: depends on nationality — Mauritius is visa-free/VoA for ~147 countries. Complete the free online "All-in-One" travel form. Passport valid 6+ months.
